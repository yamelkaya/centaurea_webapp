Solutions design and development.

Centaureа team has been dealing with business systems of different levels and complexity. Our clients vary from lifestyle businesses and start-ups to the enterprise level companies and global market leaders. In order to find the best solutions at the market we pay great attention to the development of architecture and the use of contemporary practices and modern technologies that help your business to stay on top even among the toughest competitors.

Over the last years we have developed different kind of solutions:

  • Systems for web-stores and e-commerce
  • Systems for storing, processing and analysis large volumes of social media data (Twitter, Facebook, etc)
  • Geo-location data processing systems
  • Data processing and analytics systems for mobile platforms
  • Banking systems
  • Cryptocurrency systems

By paying great attention to the architecture, constantly searching and experimenting we have obtained system engineering experience, based on such models as:

  • Pub/Sub
  • Client-Server
  • Event-Driven Architecture
  • Service Oriented Architecture (SOA)
  • CQRS
  • Event Sourcing
  • Lambda Architecture
  • N-Tier design and others

Quality is an integral attribute of our systems that is why TDD/BDD is our usual everyday practice.

For our solutions we use

NoSQL databases (such as MongoDB, DynamoDB, CouchDB, Couchbase and many others)
Distributed processing and computation systems (Apache Hadoop, Apache Storm, Apache Spark)
Message queues (RabbitMQ, MSMQ, based on ZeroMQ, Apache Kafka)
Full text search based on Lucene and ElasticSearch.
As UI development tools we use Angular.js and jQuery.
The main programming languages we code are C#/.NET, Java, Ruby, Python, JavaScript and С/C++ for algorithms and components that require uncompromised performance.

  • Distributed scaleable high-load application
  • Cloud based application development (AWS, Azure)

MongoDB as a service

Having great working experience with MongoDB database, we are ready to provide our clients with such a unique offer as “MongoDB as a Service”. No longer you have to think about data scaling, validity, backups and many other things. By virtue of our professional knowledge we provide you with MongoDB, which simply works. We are also ready to support and ensure the smooth operation of all your systems and infrastructures. Entrust your data and systems to our specialists. Contact us for further information.

Corporate trainings

Possessing great expertise in the area of modern technologies, we are ready to teach your team how to use them, share the best practices and go through the typical problems and development scenarios.

The thing that makes out trainings stand out is our exclusive approach that takes into account the needs of each particular client, characteristics of its’ team and scope of client’s challenges.

Highly qualified dedicated team and CTO/VP of Development

Centaureа is ready to offer you a team of highly qualified professionals possessing necessary skills and deep knowledge needed to achieve the client`s goals while spending reasonable amount of resources.

Dedicated team model allows companies to hire their own software development staff and manage them directly and it’s ideal for any company who needs more developers and IT professionals, but is unable to hire them because of variety of different reasons.

If you do not have the required staff at the moment, we are ready to personally build the team you need (this option is available in case of a long-term cooperation only).

We are also ready to offer you “CTO/VP of development as a service”. We understand how difficult it is to find a specialist possessing extraordinary technical skills and deep knowledge of the IT area who can take your processes to a whole new level and improve the client`s team efficiency. That is why you can employ some of our topnotch specialists who possess unchallenged knowledge, loyalty and responsibility. The offer is limited and is available in case of a long-term relationship only.
